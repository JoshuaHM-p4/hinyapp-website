Features

HinyList

Have difficulties with school, work, or productivity? Hinylists is an easy-to-use task tracker and organizer.

HinyFacts

Lightheartedly learn about your skills, environment, and self. These therapist-reviewed and research backed-learning packets can help with various life challenges.

HinyTalks

Secure, end-to-end encrypted texting allows you to connect, communicate, and receive calls with friends, individuals, and freely listen to counselors.

HinyFriends

Share thoughts and feelings, play with Hinya's features, and become friends with peers.

HinyKits is a package of features for HinyApp that provides more insights and feedback on your psychosocial health, resources for additional support, and recommendations, e-books and audiobooks to help you cope and improve your overall health, comprehensive meditation sessions and lifestyle plans to relax and relieve stress, and much more!
